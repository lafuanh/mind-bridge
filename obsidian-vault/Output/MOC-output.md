## Qualify checklist (Claude uses this)
A dump entry qualifies as a blog post if it:
- [ ] Has a clear perspective or argument
- [ ] Would be interesting to someone outside my head
- [ ] Connects to at least one bigger idea
- [ ] Is written in my voice (not generic)
- [ ] Length: 300–1500 words when expanded
