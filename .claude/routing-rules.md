## Hard Rules

- One daily planner page per day — check before create
- Manually written tasks are sacred — READ then append, never overwrite
- Max 3 grind tasks per day in TO DO (🔥 only — routines don't count)
- #interrupt: 22:00 only. Sacred.
- Every Obsidian note: at least 1 [[backlink]]
- Life/* entries: timestamped YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM WIB, every sync
- Verify all routes before strikethrough
